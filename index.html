When In Metro Manila

Safe Pass

Customers entering a food establishment in Robinsons Galleria wait in line to get their QR Codes or Safe Passes scanned. These Safe Passes are part of the  contact-tracing initiatives being implemented in the city. 

MOA Eye

Visitors can enjoy a fun and thrilling ride on the MOA Eye, a ferris wheel located at the SM Mall of Asia.

Enhanced Security

Visitors entering Robinsons Galleria are scanned for explosives and firearms by archway metal detectors stationed at all entrances and exits.

Benefits of a Smart City

There are several perks that come with making a city Smarter.  Strate Education cited the following advantages of a Smart City.

Efficient Distribution of Resources

Smart Cities are well-organized with good infrastructure. All the sectors conribute harmoniously towards making life convenient for the people.

Seamless Communication

Data gathering across Smart Cities is highly efficient. This gives the city an invaluable linfe of communication between citizens and the policy-makers who can address their needs.

Speed of Implementation

Points of improvement in Smart Cities are easily idenfitied, making changes easier to implement.

Reduced Consumption 

Optimization of water management, transportation, and power grids reduce waste by cutting down on senseless usage of resources. 

Less Carbon Emmissions

Re-distribution of power and effective transportation systems in Smart Cities help cut down on Carbon emmissions.

Reduced Power Plant Emmissions

The energy supply is reduced in areas with low demand and increased in areas with high demand, thereby reducing the overall power consumption and by extension, power plant emmissions.
